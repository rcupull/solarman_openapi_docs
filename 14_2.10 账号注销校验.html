2.10 Account cancellation verification

View on Yuque
Before canceling your account, you need to verify whether the following conditions are met at the same time:
a. There is no power station under the merchant. You need to delete all the power station information under the merchant you manage, including the power stations created by the merchant itself and the power stations authorized to the merchant;
b. There is no device under the merchant. If the merchant you manage uses the device library function, you need to delete all device information under the device library;
c. There is no organizational structure and members under the merchant. You need to delete all subordinate organizations and member information under the managed merchant;
d. There is no power station under your account, and you need to delete all the power station information under your account, including the power stations you created and the power stations authorized to you;

If satisfied, you can successfully call "Interface 2.11 Account Logout" to complete the account logout action; otherwise, the logout will not be possible.
