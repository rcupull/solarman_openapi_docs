2.11 Account cancellation

View on Yuque
After the account is canceled, your information on the platform (Your identity information, account information, and all usage records, including but not limited to historical data, alarm records, work orders, maintenance records, transaction records, etc.) will be cleared and cannot be retrieved,Please proceed with caution!
